Farewell, Friends

As of July 1, 2018, the Beegit SaaS platform has closed down. This means there is no longer a log in for existing users and no way for new users to sign up.

Thank you again to everyone who has used the product and shared their wonderful stories of writing in it over the last four years. We're grateful for the experience and wish you all happy writing as you go forward!
